I'm Lisa Woolfson and I'm a journalism and government major. I love to tell stories about underrepresented communities in any medium I can. One of my favorite videos I've made is "Queen Anne's Hall-Hate Crime Incident" which is about a hate bias incident against the LGBTQ+ community that happened at the University of Maryland. One of my favorite stories I've written is "Food insecure Jewish students lack affordable, kosher dining options" which uncovers an issue many people don't know about.
